Formula Rossa:

Formula Rossa is in Ferrari World, Abu Dhabi in the United Arab Emirates. It opened on November 4, 2010. The highest speed is 239 km/h or 149mph which it can get to in 5 seconds. The hight is 52m or 171ft. The highest drop is 51.1m or 169ft, which is most of its hight. The roller coster is 2,070m or 6,790ft long.

Steel Dragon 2000

Located in Nagashima Land amusement park in Mie Prefecture in Nagashima, Japan. The Steel Dragon 2000 was made is August 1, 2000 the year of the dragon in asia hence it's name. The Steel Dragon is known as the longest coster in the world as of right know. It is 2,479m or 8,133ft and as high as 97m or 318ft. It can reach speeds up to 153km/h or 95mph.The highest drop is 93.5m or 307ft.

The Smiler

The Smiler is located in Alton Towers Staffordshire, in The United Kingdom. It opened on May 31, 2013 after many delays. Reaching 30m or 98.4ft it drops back down, reachinf 85km/h or 52.8mph. With 14 inverisons The Smiler holds the world recored for the most inverisons.
